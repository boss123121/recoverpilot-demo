The recovery problem

Most abandoned cart tools treat hesitation like a notification problem.

Fashion buyers hesitate for emotional and practical reasons. Size, fit, trust, delivery timing, price, and confidence all change what should happen next.

Sending more reminders is not the same as recovering more revenue.

RecoverPilot turns abandoned carts into decisions: who matters, what to say, whether it is safe to send, and whether the order should be counted as recovered.

Fit uncertaintySize-based products need reassurance, not pressure.
Margin damageDiscounts are useful sometimes, but expensive as a default.
Weak priorityMerchants need to know which carts deserve action first.
No explanationAutomation without audit makes revenue claims hard to trust.
01

Generic reminders miss the real reason customers hesitate.

A dress abandoned after size selection is different from jeans abandoned at payment. RecoverPilot is designed to read the recovery context before recommending a message.

02
